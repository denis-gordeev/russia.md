Lifestyle pages should focus on habits rather than clichés. In Russia, seasonality is fundamental: daylight shifts, heating seasons, transport patterns, food preservation, and weekend movement all change with the calendar.

The dacha is not just a country house. It sits at the intersection of family economy, leisure, gardening, memory, and escape from the city. The banya is similarly more than a sauna; it is a social ritual with its own tempo, etiquette, and regional texture.
